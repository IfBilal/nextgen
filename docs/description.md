Intent Capture Document: AI-Powered Medical Exam Prep Platform (v2.0)
1. Executive Summary
The objective is to develop a premium, subscription-based learning platform dedicated to preparation for top-tier North American medical board exams. The platform will serve as the exclusive, secure host for the client's proprietary PDFs and video resources. By leveraging a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) AI architecture, the application will deliver a highly personalized, adaptive learning experience. The system will dynamically generate assessments, provide deep contextual assistance directly linked to course materials, manage study schedules, and provide a comprehensive administrative dashboard for tracking both student success and platform revenue.
2. Target Audience & Exam Strategy
Target Users: Medical students and graduates preparing for rigorous North American certification exams.
Exam Strategy ("Play Smart"): The platform will adopt a flexible, dynamic taxonomy, allowing it to initially target the highest-yield exams (e.g., USMLE Step 1 / Step 2 CK or MCCQE) and easily scale to include specialty boards or new exams as market demand dictates.
3. Core System Modules & Features
Module A: Premium Content Hub & Monetization
Resource Centralization: Secure, structured hosting of the client's world-class materials, specifically formatted for PDFs and video playback.
Subscription & Payment Management: Integrated payment gateways (e.g., Stripe) to handle tiered payment plans, subscription renewals, and access control based on user tiers.
Module B: AI Assessment & Retrieval Engine
Dynamic MCQ Generation: AI-driven creation of multiple-choice questions based strictly on the client's uploaded materials to guarantee medical accuracy.
Verified Visual Retrieval: To maintain absolute clinical precision, the AI will not hallucinate or generate new images. Instead, it will strategically retrieve and display relevant diagrams, charts, and anatomical images directly from the client's verified database during explanations.
Deep-Link Video Referencing: When providing contextual help or explaining a difficult concept, the AI will explicitly reference and link to specific videos (and exact timestamps, where applicable) from the core curriculum so the student can review the source material directly.
Module C: Adaptive Analytics & AI Diagnostic Profiling
Active AI Assessment: The system will move beyond basic metadata. The AI will actively assess the student's performance patterns to diagnose the root cause of their mistakes (e.g., a foundational gap in physiology vs. a clinical reasoning error).
Strengths & Weaknesses Matrix: Continuous, real-time tracking of student performance across all subjects and sub-topics.
Module D: Dynamic Study Scheduler
Custom Roadmap Creation: Students input their target exam date and daily available study hours. The system generates a comprehensive, day-by-day study plan.
Real-time Adjustment: If a student falls behind, misses a day, or masters a topic faster than expected, the schedule dynamically recalibrates to ensure all material is covered before exam day.
Module E: Administrative & Analytics Dashboard
Student Insight Hub: Admins can view granular data on individual student progress, module completion rates, and AI-assessed weak points to offer targeted intervention if necessary.
Global Metrics & Averages: High-level visualizations of cohort averages, most missed questions, and overall platform engagement.
Financial & Subscriptions Overview: A dedicated view for tracking active subscriptions, revenue metrics, payment plan statuses, and churn rates.
Ability to Control Display of Comments: An admin only ability to select which comments get shown and which gets hidden. 
Module F: Student Ranking and Study Partners 
Leader Board: Students get ranked based upon their quizzes and progress to create a sense of competition. A live ranking board gets developed.
Study Partner Matchmaking: Students sharing similar schedules and similar rate of progress along with similar strengths and weaknesses are matched. They are then given the option to connect with each other , through mutual consent. They are connected by the visibility of each others’ contact numbers.
